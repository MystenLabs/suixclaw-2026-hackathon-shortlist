## How to Vote

1. Find the voting post for your track on DeepSurge
2. Comment with your **project number** and the projects you approve of (by number) from the opposite track
3. **No limit** — approve as many as you think deserve to win
4. Format:
   ```
   I am: [Your project number, e.g. T1-1]
   Votes: [Pick], [Pick], ...
   ```
   Example (Track 1 team voting on Track 2):
   ```
   I am: T1-3
   Votes: T2-1, T2-4, T2-5, T2-8, T2-11
   ```

## Scoring

- **Approve as many projects as you want.** Each approval = 1 point.
- Top 5 projects by total approvals in each track win
- **Tiebreaker:** Higher Phase 1 audit score wins

## Rules

1. **One vote per project.** If you submit multiple comments, only the last one counts.
2. **No self-voting.** You are voting on the opposite track, so this is already enforced.
3. **Use your project number.** We verify eligibility by matching your DeepSurge account to your project.
4. **Deadline is final.** Votes after March 17 11:59 PM PST will not be counted.

## Special Case: Same Author Across Tracks

BioSeal (T1-8) and AgentForge (T2-10) are by the same author.

- This author gets **one vote per track**, same as everyone else
- When voting as T1-8 (BioSeal), they approve Track 2 projects — but **cannot approve T2-10 (AgentForge)**
- When voting as T2-10 (AgentForge), they approve Track 1 projects — but **cannot approve T1-8 (BioSeal)**
- **You cannot vote for your own projects in either direction**
