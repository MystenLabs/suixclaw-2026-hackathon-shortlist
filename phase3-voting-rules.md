# Phase 3: Community Favourite Voting Rules

## Overview

All 55 teams vote to determine **5 Community Favourite winners** ($200 each).

**Voting Deadline:** March 17, 2026 11:59 PM PST

> Phase 3 voting runs concurrently with Phase 2. All projects can receive votes — Phase 2 winners are excluded from Community Favourite results only after Phase 2 results are finalized.

---

## Who Can Vote

- **All 55 submitted projects** may vote
- This includes shortlisted projects — everyone votes

## Who Can Receive Votes

- **Any project** can receive votes during the voting period
- After Phase 2 results are finalized, the **10 Phase 2 track winners** (Top 5 from each track) are removed from the Community Favourite tally
- The top 5 remaining projects by total approvals win Community Favourite

## How to Vote

1. Find the Community Favourite voting post on DeepSurge
2. Comment with your **project number** and the projects you approve of
3. **Approve as many projects as you want** — no limit, except you cannot vote for yourself
4. Cross-track voting is allowed
5. Format:
   ```
   I am: [Your project number, e.g. S19 or L15]
   Votes: [Pick], [Pick], [Pick], ...
   ```
   Example:
   ```
   I am: L15
   Votes: S15, S16, L7, L19, L25
   ```

## Scoring

- **Approve as many projects as you want.** Each approval = 1 point.
- After Phase 2 winners are determined, those 10 projects are removed from the tally
- Top 5 remaining projects by total approvals win Community Favourite
- **Tiebreaker:** Higher Phase 1 audit score wins

## Special Case: Authors With Multiple Projects

Some authors submitted more than one project. These authors vote **once per project** (i.e., they submit two separate ballots). However, they **cannot vote for any of their own projects** from either ballot.

| Author | Project 1 | Project 2 |
|--------|-----------|-----------|

| @mememan | Shield Claw (S23) | TaskHawk (L22) |
| @olympusxvn | SealClaw (S21) | TRIPLE HELIX VAULT (L23) |

**Example:** Author A has Bioseal (S5) and AgentForge (L1). They submit two votes:

```
I am: S5
Votes: S19, L7, L21, L25
```

```
I am: L1
Votes: S6, S15, L8, L19
```

Neither ballot can include S5 or L1 (their own projects).

## Rules

1. **One vote per project.** If you submit multiple comments for the same project, only the last one counts.
2. **No self-voting.** You cannot vote for any of your own projects — this applies across all your submissions.
3. **Cross-track voting allowed.** You can pick projects from either track.
4. **Phase 2 winners excluded post-hoc.** All projects can receive votes, but once Phase 2 winners are determined, those winners are removed from the Community Favourite results. Votes cast for them simply don't count toward the final tally.
5. **Deadline is final.** Votes after March 17 11:59 PM PST will not be counted.
